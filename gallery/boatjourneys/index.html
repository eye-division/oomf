Heritage Boat Journeys

From Saturday June 19th to Saturday June 26th 2004, Oxford Open Water Festival 2004 celebrated the rivers, canals and streams that crisscross the county. A week-long festival brought together Oxfordshire Heritage Boat Journeys, over 60 creative events, waterways leisure and sports pursuits, environmental and science issues, art installations and free entertainment.
